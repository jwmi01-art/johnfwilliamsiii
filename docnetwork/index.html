01 — The Company

Why DocNetwork
Is the Real Deal

DocNetwork is the only camp management platform with a fully integrated EHR system — covering health records, registration, medication tracking, emergency response, and behavioral health in one place. The flagship products, CampDoc and SchoolDoc, serve 1,000+ organizations across camps, YMCAs, K–12 schools, universities, and child care centers worldwide.

Founded in 2009 by Dr. Michael Ambrose in Ann Arbor, MI, the company holds the only triple-certification in the space — HIPAA, FERPA, and SOC 2 Type 2 — a non-negotiable for any organization handling children's protected health data. They're ranked #1 on G2 for camp management software and are a 10× recipient of SPARK's FastTrack Award for Ann Arbor's fastest-growing companies.

The growth trajectory is real. Revenue is estimated at $50–100M, they've expanded into behavioral health modules and strategic partnerships, and sales headcount is actively growing — which is where this role sits.

Elevator Pitch

DocNetwork keeps kids healthy and safe while they're away from home — and does it with the only purpose-built EHR platform in the youth program market. The mission is real, the market position is defensible, and the growth curve is steep. That's a combination worth showing up for.
